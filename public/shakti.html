Our Story

Woven with Heritage,
Sold with Honesty

For over 35 years, Shivashakti Pattu Sarees has been a trusted name in authentic silk weaving. Based in the heart of Weavers Colony, Hindupur, we operate our own in-house looms — ensuring every saree that leaves our workshop carries the soul of true craftsmanship.

Because we sell directly to customers and wholesalers — eliminating middlemen — we offer prices 30–40% lower than market rates, without ever compromising on quality.
